Zomato is a prominent Indian multinational restaurant aggregator and food delivery platform. It connects users with local dining establishments by offering services like restaurant discovery, menu viewing, user-generated reviews, table reservations, and on-demand doorstep food delivery.Zomato has expanded its platform into a broader food-tech ecosystem, featuring several key services:

Food Delivery: Browse thousands of local restaurants, place orders directly through the app, and track deliveries in real time.Dining Out: Discover nearby restaurants, read customer reviews, book tables, and find exclusive deals or loyalty memberships like Zomato Gold.Hyperpure: A B2B platform that supplies fresh ingredients and kitchen supplies directly to restaurant partners.Quick Commerce & Other Verticals: Through its subsidiary Blinkit, Zomato also provides instant grocery and household item delivery.
